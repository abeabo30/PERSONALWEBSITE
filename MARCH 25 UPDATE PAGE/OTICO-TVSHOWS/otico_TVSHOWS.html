Phineas and Ferb

Phineas and Ferb, an animated comedy series, follows the adventures of Phineas Flynn and his English stepbrother, Ferb Fletcher, on their summer vacation. Each day, the boys and their friends take on some ambitious new project, sometimes to help someone in need, but often just to create something fun.

Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is an American animated media franchise and series of comedy short films created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. Best known for its 161 theatrical short films by Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, the series centers on the rivalry between the titular characters of a cat named Tom and a mouse named Jerry.

Start Up

Seo Dal Mi has dreams of becoming Korea's own Steve Jobs, and with her genius first love, an investor, and a business insider by her side, her dream may be closer than she thinks. Its refreshingly unique concept delves deeper into the startup world, dreams, the rocky path to success, and everything in between—family, love, and friendships.

Hi Bye, Mama!

"Hi Bye, Mama!" is a South Korean television drama series that aired in 2020. The story revolves around Cha Yu-ri, a ghost who gets a second chance at life. After passing away in a tragic accident, Yu-ri becomes a ghost and watches over her husband and daughter from the afterlife. However, when she is given the opportunity to return to the human world for 49 days.

Solo Leveling

"Solo Leveling" is a popular South Korean web novel series written by Chu-Gong and illustrated by Jang Sung-Rak. It follows the story of Sung Jin-Woo, a weak hunter who gains the ability to level up like a video game character after entering a mysterious dungeon.

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

"Frieren: Beyond Journey's End" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Kanehito Yamada. It tells the story of Frieren, an elf who embarks on a journey to find her human friend, who has passed away.
