Childhood Years

I am Gabriel N. Otico, born on June 20, 2004, at Angono Medics Hospital. The youngest of five siblings, I grew up facing health challenges due to asthma1. Despite these difficulties, my childhood was filled with unique experiences that shaped my perspective on life2. From a young age, my weak immune system led to frequent hospitalizations for asthma-related issues3. This not only affected my physical activities but also limited my interactions with others4. I often found myself indoors, playing alone or occasionally with neighborhood friends5. The struggle to breathe during physical activities made me envy other children who could play without worry6. The isolation and limitations sometimes made me feel lonely, especially when comparing my experiences to those of other kids7. However, I discovered solace in the simple joys of being at home8. Playing with my parents and siblings became a source of happiness, teaching me that connection and love could triumph over physical limitations9. Looking back, I realize the financial strain my health placed on my family10.

Hospital bills were a burden, and I vividly remember my parents searching for ways to cover these expenses11. Despite our limited resources, they went to great lengths, even seeking help from others to ensure I received the medical care I needed12. I idolized my parents during those challenging times13. Their unwavering support and sacrifices became evident as they worked tirelessly to provide for my needs14. I remember the comfort they brought me, especially through my favorite indulgence, Jollibee, which became a symbol of love and care during those difficult days15. My childhood was a mix of challenges and heartwarming moments16. While health issues created hurdles, the love and support of my family became the foundation of my resilience17. Today, I cherish those early experiences, recognizing the strength and unity that emerged from overcoming adversity18. These trials not only shaped my character but also instilled in me a profound appreciation for the intangible riches of life – the warmth of family bonds, the enduring power of love, and the importance of resilience in the face of adversity19. As I navigate through life, the lessons learned in those formative years continue to guide me, reminding me of the invaluable treasures found in the midst of struggle and the unwavering strength that family provides20.

Teenage Years

As I entered my teenage years, a pivotal period of self-discovery and growth, I found myself focusing on academics1. High school became a significant chapter marked by challenges, determination, and unexpected opportunities2. During high school, I faced criticism from the parents of my classmates who belittled my academic abilities3. Determined to prove them wrong, I committed myself to rigorous studying for every quiz and examination4. Through hard work and perseverance, I not only met but exceeded expectations, achieving high scores in various subjects5. My high school days are etched in my memory as a time of self-discovery and resilience6. Despite the academic challenges, I cherished the friendships formed during this time7. I had a circle of friends with whom I could share dreams and aspirations8. Together, we navigated the complexities of adolescence, supporting each other through shared experiences9. An unexpected opportunity arose when an application for a C.A.T Officer opened from Grade 8 to Grade 1010.

Despite initial skepticism from classmates due to perceived physical limitations, I decided to challenge myself11. Joining turned out to be the best decision of my life, proving to myself and others that strength comes in various forms12. This experience became a turning point, marking my transition from the perceived 'weak kid' to a capable individual13. Transitioning to senior high school coincided with the onset of the pandemic14. Adapting to a new setup at STI Ortigas-Cainta Branch under the ICT strand presented unique challenges, particularly in connecting with classmates through online meetings15. Despite the initial difficulties, I found myself elected as a class president16. This role, though unfamiliar, became a platform for personal growth, honing my communication skills, and teaching me valuable leadership lessons17. Reflecting on my teenage years, I see a journey marked by resilience, personal growth, and the determination to overcome challenges18. Academic triumphs, unexpected leadership roles, and enduring friendships have shaped my identity and prepared me for the next chapters of life19. As I move forward, I carry with me the lessons learned during these formative years, grateful for the strength and resilience that emerged from each experience20.

College Years

As I embarked on my college journey at the University of the East - Manila, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), I knew that this phase would bring me closer to the real world, equipping me with the skills and knowledge needed in the industry1. Intrigued not only by my sister's success in the same course but also by my passion for gaming, I eagerly embraced the challenges that lay ahead2. While my love for gaming initially fueled my interest in BSIT, I soon realized the inherent difficulty of the course3. Determined to turn this challenge into motivation, I committed myself to excelling in my studies4. The journey was tough, but my passion and newfound determination pushed me to overcome obstacles and thrive in a field that constantly demanded growth and adaptability5. Fortunately, I discovered a supportive circle of friends who became my pillars during the toughest times6. Their encouragement and friendship transformed challenges into shared victories, creating a sense of community that made the college experience not only academically rewarding but personally enriching as well7. Looking back from my leadership experiences in high school, I found that my communication skills played a crucial role in connecting with my peers8. I effortlessly assigned tasks, facilitated collaboration, and ensured a smooth workflow within our group9.

These skills, honed in high school, became invaluable assets in navigating the complexities of group projects and assignments10. As I navigate through my second year, the upcoming thesis/capstone project waits ahead11. This challenge, though daunting, fuels my determination to not only survive but thrive in my academic pursuits12. Looking five years into the future, I envision myself as a professional in the IT industry13. I firmly believe that with unwavering dedication, a willingness to learn, and the application of acquired skills, every challenge becomes an opportunity for growth and success14. In essence, my college journey has been a transformative period, pushing me beyond my comfort zone and molding me into a resilient and determined individual15. With the support of friends, the lessons learned from leadership experiences, and an unwavering commitment to my goals16. I look forward to embracing the challenges that lie ahead, I’m hoping that my college years are laying the foundation for a successful and fulfilling professional future in the world of Information Technology17. Life is like a roller coaster; full of twists and turns, ups and downs, and full of surprises18. But when life's journey is smooth, life is beautiful19. I'm looking forward for my journey that waits ahead20.
